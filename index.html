Hello World !

I am a 5thyear PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore MD. I work with Dan Povey and Sanjeev Khudanpur at the Center for Language and Speech Processing in the general area of speech processing. I am currently focusing on semi-supervised and unsupervised training of acoustic and language models, and speech segmentation for automatic speech recognition. My contributions to the open source Kaldi speech recognition toolkit can be found on my github page. To know more about my research and publications, follow the links at the top of my page.

Previously, I got my B.Tech degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Madras. For my Bachelor's thesis, I worked with Umesh S on acoustic models for ASR.


Research Interests

Automatic Speech Recognition, Machine Learning, Speech Processing, Natural Language Processing
